FACILITATED THE FORUM OF
SPECIAL HEAD TEACHERS
ASSOCIATION –KENYA (SSHAK).

SSHAK is a national association of head teachers of Special
schools and teachers from special units. SSHAK’s clear
mandate is to lobby for the agenda of learners with Special
needs and monitor government policy implementation in line
with Education Act 2013 Chapter 6. SSHAK’s current focus as
mentioned in the meeting by the Chairman Mr Arthur
Imjenga was working with the government on securing
special needs funds and support within public primary and
secondary school at local level.He also added that it was
important that the stated grants for special units are accessed
for specified and indentified learners and utilized for them.

ANNUAL NATIONAL SPECIAL SPORTS
13TH – 19TH APRIL 2014 EMBU –KENYA

Maisha Kara through the kind support of Inoradopt’s staff was able to participate in
this year’s annual national special supports activities on 13th -19th April in Embu,
Kenya. The euro 1,190 received from Inoradopt was facilitated the following activities at
the national special Sports in Embu;

1. Participation of 3 MK volunteers in the sports events
2. Provided refreshments for 66 children (deaf/blind & Albino), 10 teachers and 5
support staff.
3. Facilitated the forum of Special Head Association –Kenya (SSHAK).

PARTICIPATION OF 3 MK VOLUNTEERS IN THE SPORTS EVENT
Maisha Kara in the previous year through the support of Inoradopt had provided T-Shirts and medals for 16 deaf/blind learners and T-Shirt for their 15 carers and 10 teachers.
